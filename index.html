Information We Collect

We may collect personal and technical information when users interact with our services.

How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and student support requests

  • Send updates regarding admissions and schedules
  • Improve chatbot and automated support systems
  • Contact interested students for follow-up
03

Facebook Messenger & Automation

Community Skills Institute uses Facebook Messenger automation to communicate with users efficiently.

  • Automated replies may respond to common questions
  • Users may voluntarily provide contact details
  • Conversation data may be stored securely for support purposes
  • Messenger usage is also governed by Meta’s Privacy Policy
We do not sell or misuse Messenger conversation data for advertising or unauthorized marketing purposes.
